Description

Our school is one of the best and most esteemed schools in Arizona! While we are somewhat limited on what we offer
from the people that have taken our classes we have accumulated an amazing 1/5 stars! We offer classes mostly for
anyone wanting to perform in a cicus but these skills can be used in other places as well! All of our classes are
the best of qualities and they provide a lot of real life experience and teach valuable lessons in this career. Now
this might sound too good to be true, but we do have one issue, our costs ot get in are quite high and we only have
a 107% accepetance rate.


History

East-Mec was founded in Arizona in 1914 at the start of WWI so that people could have entertainment while everyone
else was at war. It didn't go well and most of the people were made fun of for being cowards that wouldn't fight in
the war. We slowly expanded around the area of Arizona and we are now the biggest circus school in the state of
Arizona. We are very presltigous and have a beutiful history of successful performers!
